About Me


Razu Mohiuddin is a graduate student in Physics at Case Western Reserve University. Razu is a member of the Monreal Lab and the Project 8 collaboration, where he is conducting research in the exciting field of neutrino physics.

He received his Bachelor's degree in Physics from University of Dhaka. During this time he was a member of Community of Physics and conducted research on electromagnetism and gravitational wave as part of the oraganization.
