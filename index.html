Hi!

I am an experimental particle astrophysics doctoral student at Case Western Reserve University. I'm currently working in the Monreal Lab and the Project 8 collaboration, pursuing my research interests in neutrino physics. I am passionate about understanding the fundamental properties of neutrinos and their role in the universe. My research focuses on developing new techniques to measure neutrino mass with high precision, with the goal of shedding light on some of the most pressing questions in particle physics and cosmology. Through my work, I hope to contribute to our understanding of the origins of matter and the nature of the universe. I am also interested in optics, astrophysics, gravitation and machine learning. As a scientist, I am committed to fostering collaboration and community in the pursuit of scientific discovery.
Please feel free to get in touch if you have any questions!
